Sudoku

Instructions

Your goal is to fill in every missing cell number. To fill in a cell, you click one to select and then click one of the numbered buttons below the board to input the desired number. If your choice was wrong, the cell number will be colored red. If your choice was correct, the cell number will be colored green. If all inputed numbers are green, you win; if one is red, you lose.

Controls

  • Undo: takes back your previous move
  • Redo: takes back your undone move
  • Solve: immediately solves the entire board
  • Numeric keypress (1 to 9): inputs the number pressed into the selected cell

Sudoku Rules

  1. Each row must contain numbers from 1 to 9 with no repetitions
  2. Each column must contain all numbers from 1 to 9 with no repetitions
  3. Each 3x3 subgrid must contain all numbers from 1 to 9 with no repetitions
